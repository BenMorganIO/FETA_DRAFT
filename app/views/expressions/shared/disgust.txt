For disgust, the eyebrows lower (Procerus, Depressor Supercilli, and Corrugator muscles [AU4]) and the nose wrinkles (Levator Labii Superioris and Levator Labii Superioris Alaeque Nasi muscles [AU9]). The cheek will rise causing multiple lines below the eyes (Orbicularis Oculi Pars Orbitalis muscle [AU7]). The upper lip will rise (Levator Labii Superioris and Caput Infraorbitalis muscles [AU10]) causing the notable snarl that occurs with the lower lip being motioned forward and either being raised or lowered (Orbicularis Oris muscle [AU16]).
